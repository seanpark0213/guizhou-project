The Guizhou Project is a student-led initiative dedicated to supporting the underdeveloped communities in Guizhou through service and education. Join us in making a meaningful impact.

About Us

We are a group of passionate students committed to social responsibility. Each year, we visit Guizhou to provide essential support through teaching, donations, and community engagement. The project fosters mutual understanding and lasting change.
